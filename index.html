About Me

The Architect
Behind the Vision

I am Sahil Akhtar Hawari, an architecture student driven by the belief that great spaces shape human experience. My work explores the intersection of structural integrity and poetic form — buildings that are not just seen, but felt. From urban housing concepts to cultural pavilions, each project is a dialogue between context, materiality, and purpose.
